About The Review Co.

Who are we?

We are a team of enthusiasts working on make the product review process simple. We are focused to capture the first expression of the users as we all know first expression is the last expression. Currently, we are in beta phase and we are offering our Services for free. We would like to invite everyone to try our review system.

What we Provide?

If you are a organization, we provide
  • platform to test your products
  • platform to capture the first impression thatyour product makes on the users
  • platform to get insights on the products
If you would like to be an reviewer, we provide
  • platform where you can get info on the products that are currently on the testing phase
  • platform to earn some incentives on different brand products

What do we charge?

Currently we have just opened our services. So, currently we provide services free of cost. The services for our reviewers will always be free. But at later point of time, the organizations wil have to pay a basic fee that will be decided at later point of time.
